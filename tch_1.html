Excerpt from...

The Cursed Heirs

By Mahi Sheth


She walked towards the throne room, smiling, as torches held by iron sconces casted shadows that seemed to run away from her. The flames created an ethereal glow, illuminating intricate carvings — on the palace walls — depicting scenes of royal triumphs and devastating losses.

Arriving at the arching wooden doors, she reminded herself what this was all for. For her grandmother. For her mother. For her brother. For the betrayal they had all suffered from. She pulled up her hood, as the bottom of her black dress fluttered around to a stop. Waving out her arms, she blasted open the door. The two guards inside tensed, instinctively moving to unsheath their swords, sensing danger, yet all they saw was empty space. Her lips quivered into a slight grin, as she made her way past them, knocking them out with a flick of her hands.

Making her way towards the doors that lead to the Queen’s room, she glanced at the throne. Her throne. She paused, as a glimmer of shine bounced off the darkness behind the chair.

“Come out now child.” Thank Nythekdikeo*, she thought as she made her way toward the throne. She sighed as the child refused to come out of hiding. Crouching down a few steps away, she waved her hands in a sideways motion, moving the throne from its resting place, revealing two children huddled together, their hands joined together in death’s grip. She faltered. They were children, so innocent. What am I doing? They couldn’t have been older than ten and four. The boy on the left covered the little girl’s face, his arms wrapped around her as she slept, whispering in her ear it was alright. He had a jagged flame, dark with edges unevenly shaped on his forearm, marking him a royal of the Vorthmir Kingdom. The girl’s faint silver-blue intricate knot near her collarbone marked her as Eldrathis Kingdom’s heir. Looking at them, she was reminded of her own brother, and all the vengeance came flooding back.

How convenient—both of my targets in one place. Her lips curled into a smirk as she stepped forward. "Tell me, boy, do you even know who she is? She’s the daughter of your enemy—the princess of Eldrathis.”



*Nythekdikeo — God of Vengeance
